Privacy Policy

Daily Balance · Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Daily Balance is a personal productivity app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your Google profile photo

We do not collect any other personal data.

How We Use Your Data

  • To identify you when you sign in
  • To display your name and photo in the app
  • To back up your routine data to your personal Google Drive

Data Storage

All your routine data (schedule, notes, goals, habits) is stored:

  • Locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • In your own Google Drive — only you can access it

We do not store your data on any external server.

Google Drive Access

We request access to your Google Drive only to save and restore your backup file. We use the drive.appdata scope — this means we can only access files created by this app, not your personal Drive files.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Google Drive API (backup only)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

Your Rights

  • You can sign out at any time from Settings
  • You can delete your backup from Google Drive at any time
  • You can clear all local data from Settings → Reset All Data
